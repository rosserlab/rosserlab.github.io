Resources

https://heliconius-maps.github.io/

A website providing interactive maps for visualising the phenotypes and geographic distributions of Heliconius and related genera, along with a portal for downloading the underlying data. This underlying data was first published by Rosser et al. 2012, and the mapping framework along with additional data subsequently published in Rosser and Mallet (2024).

Rosser and Mallet (2024) Tropical Lepidoptera Research In press.
Rosser et al. (2012) Biological Journal of the Linnean Society 105:479-497.

Tweets papers about Heliconius and related genera when they appear on PubMed or bioRxiv.
